Google Play Store App Analysis¶

by Harutaka Kawamura¶

Dataset Overview¶

This dataset contains all the details of the applications on Google Play. There are 13 features that describe a given app such as category, rating, installs, etc.

Investigation Overview¶

The overall goal of this analysis is find interesting relationships or correlations between the features.

Rating distribution¶

  • Average rating is 4.17.
  • 53% apps are rated higher than the average rating(4.17).

App size distribution by categories¶

  • For most of categories, app sizes skew to the right.
  • game has a size different distribution where the number of light apps(< 20MB) is smaller compared to other categories. This is probably because games tend to require a lot of assets(image, sound, etc.) and codes to implement complex features.

Rating vs Reviews for free and paid apps¶

  • The relationship between rating and reviews is similar (positive correlation) for free and paid apps.
  • Paid apps have less reivews than free apps. This is not surprising because people are more likely to install free apps.
